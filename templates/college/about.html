About

National College, founded in 1999, is managed by "BODHI NIKETAN TRUST", formed by the members of St. Joseph Province of the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI).
The institution strives to fulfill its mission to provide educational
opportunities for all aspiring young people to excel in life by developing academic excellence,
fostering values, creating civic responsibility, inculcating environmental concern and building global competencies in a dynamic environment.
